03 / The Customer

Who's on the
other end of the line.

Small and mid-sized American manufacturers. The owner, the plant manager, the maintenance supervisor. People who care about uptime, audit documentation, and not getting gouged by the premium brands.

Core Segment

CNC Machine Shops

Aerospace (AS9100), medical device (ISO 13485), automotive (IATF 16949). These shops are contractually required to use UL / CSA certified equipment. Our certifications are their audit documentation.

Fabrication & Welding

Metal Shops

High continuous air demand, sensitive to compressor downtime. A failed compressor shuts the whole floor. They care about reliability and parts availability.

Auto Body & Collision

Paint + Frame Shops

Clean dry air is a quality requirement. Paint booth contamination from moisture and oil equals callbacks. They buy desiccant dryers, not just refrigerated.

Food & Beverage

Processing Facilities

Require oil-free or food-grade air. USDA / FDA compliance drives the purchase decision. Higher tier dryers and activated carbon filters.

Larger Manufacturing

Plants Running 50 HP+

Energy cost is the hook. A permanent magnet VFD compressor saves 30 to 40 percent on electricity versus a fixed-speed unit. That's real money at the plant level.

Refugees

Tired Quincy & IR Customers

Shops that have been paying dealer markup for years and are ready to hear a better story. Same certifications, same quality, 20 to 40 percent less money.
